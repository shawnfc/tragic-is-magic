Tragic is Magic seeks to guarantee that graduating high school seniors and students in a four year who are aging out of foster care have the necessary funds to attend and complete post-secondary education. Tragic Is Magic is a scholarship fund aimed at giving foster youth a chance to overcome tragic experiences; we offer youth an opportunity to choose a different path by continuing their success and making the journey to college graduation and a magical beginning...

Who is eligible?

You are eligible if you have been or are currently involved with San Francisco ILSP and are currently a senior in high school or attending a 4 year university. Must be between the age of 18-25 and have been eligible for foster care between your 16 th and 18 th . We also accept applications from foster youth not affiliated with SFILSP but still attaining higher education.

What can I use the money for? Funds can be used for EDUCATION RELATED COSTS only including tuition, books and basic living needs while attending college or for HOUSING ­RELATED COSTS that help you to become stable and able to support yourself.

For questions or about applications please contact Elexus Hunter at elexushunter@tragicismagic.com.
